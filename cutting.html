Cutting Guide

Lose fat safely and effectively through calorie control, smart nutrition, and consistent training.

What is Cutting?

Cutting is a fitness phase focused on reducing body fat while preserving as much muscle as possible. This is done through a controlled calorie deficit, high-protein diets, strength training, and sustainable habits. A proper cutting phase is not starvation — it’s strategic, balanced, and goal-oriented.

Calorie Deficit Basics

The Smart Deficit

A safe calorie deficit is around 300–500 calories below maintenance. Going too low slows metabolism and causes muscle loss. Proper cutting means eating enough to fuel workouts while still reducing fat slowly and safely.

Protein Priority

Protein protects your muscle during a cut. Aim for 1.6–2.2g per kg of bodyweight. More protein = better recovery, fuller meals, and stable energy levels.

Proper Cutting Meal Recipe

ZINGY CHICKEN BURGER

Ingredients

For the zingy chicken fillets:
• 80g spicy tortilla chips
• 1 tsp chilli powder
• 1 tsp garlic granules
• 1 tsp paprika
• 4 chicken breasts
• 1 egg (beaten)
• 50g cornflour
• Low-calorie cooking spray

For the burgers:
• 4 sesame seed buns
• Lettuce leaves
• 2 tomatoes (sliced)
• Sauce of choice

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C.
  2. Crush tortilla chips with chilli powder, garlic, and paprika.
  3. Prepare three bowls: cornflour, beaten egg, tortilla mix.
  4. Coat chicken: cornflour → egg → tortilla crumbs.
  5. Bake 25 mins, flip, spray, bake another 20 mins.
  6. Prepare buns with sauce, lettuce, and tomato.
  7. Add cooked chicken fillet, top with more sauce, serve.

Cutting Tips

  • Drink at least 2–3L of water daily.
  • Never remove carbs completely, choose whole grains.
  • Sleep 7–9 hours; fat loss slows without sleep.
  • Use lighter weights but maintain proper form.
  • Avoid sugary drinks at all costs.
