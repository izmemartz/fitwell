Home Workouts for Beginners

Train anytime, anywhere — no equipment or minimal equipment needed.

No-Equipment Home Workouts

Full Body – Bodyweight Squats

Guide: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Sit your hips back and bend your knees as if sitting on a chair. Push through your heels to return to standing. Perform 3 sets of 12–15 reps.

Upper Body – Incline Push-Ups

Guide: Place your hands on a table or bench. Lower your chest toward the edge while keeping your body straight. Push back up. Great for beginners. 3 sets of 8–12 reps.

Core – Plank

Guide: Keep your body straight from head to heels while resting on forearms. Hold your core tight. Hold for 20–30 seconds for beginners.

Home Workouts With Equipment

Back – Resistance Band Rows

Guide: Wrap the band around a pole. Pull toward your torso while squeezing your shoulder blades. Slowly return. 3 sets of 12–15 reps.

Shoulders – Dumbbell Front Raises

Guide: Raise dumbbells straight to shoulder height. Lower slowly. Keep elbows slightly bent. 3 sets of 10–12 reps.

Legs – Dumbbell Lunges

Guide: Step forward and lower your knee. Push through your front leg to stand. Alternate legs. 3 sets of 8–10 reps each side.
